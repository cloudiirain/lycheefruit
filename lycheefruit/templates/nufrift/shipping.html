Why are there shipping costs?

Rift Island is located in a Dimensional Rift located near the Republic of Kiribati, which is the poorest third-world island nation in the Pacific Ocean. One small government-contracted supply ship runs a 15-day circuit between Kiribati and Rift Island.

"Common goods", which are items that can be found in typical third-world supermarkets and civilian general stores (e.g. Walmart) are stocked in Kiribati and take 15 days to arive after a purchase order is placed. Please note that there is no refrigerated cargo on the supply ship.

"Rare goods" include everything else (e.g. tractor, solar generator, farm livestock, cave diving equipment, unusual things). These goods must be shipped internationally to Kiribati before they are picked up by the supply ship. It takes 30 days for rare goods to arrive after a purchase order is placed.

Standard Box/Crate Shipping Calculator

For small packages, the shipping rate is usually calculated by weight (parcel shipping). However, there eventually reaches a point where the package is heavy enough that it is cheaper to ship by volume (freight shipping).

This calculator automatically takes this into account. Please enter the gross sum of the weight and volume of everything you wish to ship.
